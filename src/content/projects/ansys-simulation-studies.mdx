## Tools & Capabilities

| Tool | Use |
|------|-----|
| ANSYS Fluent | CFD, incompressible flow, turbulence modelling (k-ε, k-ω), conjugate heat transfer, steady & transient |
| ANSYS Mechanical | FEA, static structural, thermal-structural coupling, contact analysis |
| ANSYS Meshing | Structured/unstructured mesh, body sizing, inflation layers, proximity/curvature capture |
| Post-processing | Contour plots, vector fields, streamlines, residual tracking, surface integrals (HTC, heat flux) |
